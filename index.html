Aliter

Aliter is a middle school where different persons with a different gender are situated. These people all have their own unique problem and it's your job to fix it! Or just explore the world!

Controls

use ZQSD, WASD or the joystick to walk. Mouse, tab & enter to interact, shift to run and space to emote.

Purpose

The purpose of Aliter is to get more acceptance for the lgbtq+ community and to ensure the safety of the community in public!
